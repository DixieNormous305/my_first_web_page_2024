Computer Science Degree Overview

This page provides a detailed overview of the Computer Science degree, including its purpose, structure, and a comprehensive list of subjects covered in the program.

Degree Structure

The Computer Science 4 year program is designed to equip students with foundational knowledge and practical skills in computing. Below is an overview of the subjects of different subjects we've done so far:

Semester Subject Category
1 Introduction to Programming Core
1 Discrete Mathematics Core
1 Maths for Engeneering Core
1 Fundamentals of Computer Science Core
1 Logic and Critical Thinking Core
1 Human abilities Core
1 Administration of Operative Sistems Elective
1 Introduction to Cybersecurity Elective

Subject Descriptions

  • Introduction to Programming: Learn the basics of programming in C, problem-solving, and algorithmic thinking.
  • Discrete Mathematics: Explore the mathematical foundations of computer science, including logic, set theory, and graph theory.
  • Maths for Engeneering: Maths that covers heavy subjects such as calculus, algebra and differential equations.
  • Fundamentals of Computer Engeneering: This subject aims to provide a global vision of Computer Engineering in both academic and professional points of view.
  • Human abilities: Teaches the student about the origins of University and to know ourselves.
  • Logic and Critical Thinking: This subject teaches the student to question and understand evidence in a more objective way.
  • Administration of Operative Sistems:Study the principles of operating systems, including process management, scheduling, and file systems.
  • Introduction to Cybersecurity: Understand the principles of securing computer systems and networks against threats and vulnerabilities.
